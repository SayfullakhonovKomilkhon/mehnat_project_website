# Product Context: Mehnat Kodeksiga Sharh

## Why This Project Exists

### Problem Statement
Citizens, employers, and legal professionals in Uzbekistan need accessible, authoritative information about labor laws. The Labor Code is complex, and finding clear interpretations requires legal expertise that most people don't have access to.

### Solution
**Mehnat Kodeksiga Sharh** provides:
1. Official government-verified commentary on Labor Code articles
2. Professional legal interpretations in plain language
3. Multi-language access (Uzbek, Russian, English)
4. AI-powered assistance for common questions
5. Searchable, organized structure for easy navigation

## How It Should Work

### User Journey

#### Citizens
1. **Discovery**: Find the platform through government channels
2. **Search**: Look for specific labor law topics or articles
3. **Learn**: Read official commentary and interpretations
4. **Ask**: Use AI chat for quick questions
5. **Trust**: See verification badges confirming authenticity

#### Employers
1. **Reference**: Check labor law requirements
2. **Compliance**: Understand obligations clearly
3. **Updates**: Stay informed about law changes
4. **Documentation**: Save/print relevant articles

#### Legal Professionals
1. **Research**: Deep dive into specific articles
2. **Cross-reference**: Navigate related articles
3. **Analysis**: Access expert commentary
4. **Citation**: Get authoritative source material

### User Experience Goals

#### Trust & Authority
- Government branding establishes credibility
- Verification badges confirm official status
- Clean, professional design conveys seriousness
- State emblem reinforces authority

#### Accessibility
- Mobile-responsive for all devices
- Multi-language for diverse users

#### Efficiency
- Fast search and filtering
- Clear article organization by chapters
- Quick access to related content
- AI assistance for common questions
